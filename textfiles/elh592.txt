Hymn #592 - Praise God, from Whom All Blessings Flow
Tune: OLD HUNDREDTH
File: elh592.txt

[1] Praise God, from whom all blessings flow;
    Praise Him, all creatures here below;
    Praise Him above, ye heav'nly host;
    Praise Father, Son, and Holy Ghost.
    Amen
